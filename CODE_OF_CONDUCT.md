# Code of conduct

ScreenMCP welcomes constructive participation. Be respectful, discuss technical choices in good faith,
and keep feedback specific to the work. Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, sexualized
attention, and publishing another person's private data are not accepted in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when needed to protect the
community. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security advisory form when a
public report would expose personal information. Maintainers will handle reports as privately as the
situation permits.
